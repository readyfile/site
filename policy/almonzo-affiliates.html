Almonzo Affiliate & Channel Partner Agreement

This Agreement is entered into between Readyfile Ltd ("Company") and the participating Partner.

1. Definitions

"Almonzo" means the charity management platform available at www.almonzo.org.

"Affiliate Link" means a tracking link issued via get.almonzo.org.

"New Customer" means an organisation that has not held an Almonzo subscription within the previous 24 months.

2. Non-Exclusivity

This Agreement is non-exclusive.

3. Partner Acceptance

Acceptance into the Programme is at the sole discretion of Readyfile Ltd.

4. Customer Transactions and Commission

Commission is payable only for a New Customer's initial qualifying transaction.

No commission is payable on renewals, upgrades, additional licences, future subscription periods, or subsequent purchases unless agreed in writing.

5. Commission Eligibility

  1. The lead must be valid and accepted.
  2. A qualifying customer transaction must occur.
  3. The customer must be an eligible UK charity or approved not-for-profit organisation.
  4. The customer must have paid at least 125% of the commission amount due, excluding VAT, refunds, credits and chargebacks.
  5. The customer must remain in good standing.

6. Self Referrals

No commission is payable for self-referrals or referrals involving entities controlled by the Partner.

7. Refunds and Chargebacks

Readyfile Ltd may reclaim, offset or withhold commission relating to refunded or cancelled transactions.

8. Licence

Readyfile Ltd grants a revocable, non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to promote Almonzo using authorised materials.

9. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property rights in Almonzo, branding and materials remain the property of Readyfile Ltd.

10. Confidentiality

Each party shall keep confidential information confidential except where disclosure is required by law.

11. Taxes

The Partner is responsible for all taxes, duties and fees arising from commission payments.

12. Term and Termination

Either party may terminate on 15 days written notice. Readyfile Ltd may terminate immediately for fraud, insolvency, reputational harm, or material breach.

13. Indemnity

The Partner shall indemnify Readyfile Ltd against third-party claims arising from the Partner's participation in the Programme.

14. Limitation of Liability

Neither party shall be liable for indirect or consequential losses. Liability shall be limited to commissions paid during the preceding 12 months.

15. Compliance

The Partner shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

16. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. The courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

17. General

Assignment, force majeure, notices, severability, waiver, amendment and survival provisions apply.
