About Almonzo

A Brief Introduction

Almonzo is our charity software division. It is designed for UK charities, with the following features:

  • Donation Receipt Issuance
  • Charity Membership Directory
  • Gift Aid Reports (ready for HMRC)
  • Donation Reports
  • Data Subject Access Reports
  • Gift Aid Consent Forms & Upload

Development of Almonzo

Almonzo began as a software for our founder's coursework for A Level Computer Science, and was first used in November 2023. Since the coursework submission, the software has been maintained by Readyfile, and undergone improvements, to make it better for our charities.

Oh - and if you are curious, the software gained 70 / 70 marks, after moderation, during the conferration of A Level results in 2024.
