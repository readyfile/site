About Readyfile (Accountancy Software)

A Brief Introduction

Our accountancy software is tailored towards accountancy firms, which currently supports bookkeeping functions, including extracting data from scanned invoices and bank statements, uploaded in bulk, deduplicating, and consilidation.

Whilst this is still a work in progress, with new features being developed, especially with Making Tax Digital, and with advancements in AI, it currently supports the invoice extraction.

Development of Readyfile

Readyfile began some development in 2020, with a major spike in development in 2022. Further development occurred in 2025, as AI models improved, aiding our invoice data extraction.
