Hello there! Welcome to Readyfile, or well the temporary landing page.

Readyfile is a solution to accountancy firms (mainly in the United Kingdom) where an automated software-as-a-service can process invoices, helping especially in VAT.
We also intend on adding a feature where we can link bank statements to the invoices or credit notes submitted to us, allowing accountants to have an easier time analyzing bank and cash-in-hand accounts.
